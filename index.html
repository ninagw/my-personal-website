Pocket Pets

Remember Tamagotchis? This cozy gaming app invites you to a virtual world where you can take care of your virtual pets, play minigames to earn money or visit the pet shop to buy treats, clothes and toys. A joyful design with weather changes, seasons and music makes "Pocket Pets" a real gaming experience. I created this mobile first app with two collegues of my Web Development Bootcamp 2024. In our four week group project we mainly used React.js, next.js and styled-components. Also user stories, wireframes and the agile workflow helped us to bring our vision to life.

Event Website

As part of my final project at journalism school, I set up a website using Wordpress that drew attention to our event.
